Dear Guest,

As part of the guidance received from the CDC, we’ve implemented new boarding and arrival protocols. As a result, we’ll need some extra time for check-in. We’ll now depart from Orlando (Port Canaveral), Florida at 5:00 PM, instead of 4:30 PM.

Separately, to maintain your safety and wellbeing, we’ll visit Cozumel, Mexico instead of Labadee due to the current political and security situation in Haiti. Regrettably, this impacts our visit to Falmouth, Jamaica because of its distance from Mexico. Instead, we’ll visit Costa Maya, Mexico, and we’ve swapped the order we’ll visit our private destination, Perfect Day at CocoCay and Nassau, Bahamas.

We know how much time and effort goes into planning your vacation, and we’re sorry for any inconvenience caused by this. We’re excited to take you to new ports and we know you’ll have an excellent time! Below, please find our revised Harmony of the Seas November 28th, 2021 itinerary.
